About

Remote sensing and GeoAI, grounded in fieldwork and journals

"The ingenuity of GeoAI and human insight unlocks new ways to understand Earth's features, confront its problems, and design lasting solutions."

I'm a Remote Sensing and GeoAI specialist based in Jaipur, India, focused on urban heat islands, LULC dynamics, wildlife habitat modelling, and climate-sensitive mountain systems. I work hands-on across Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, ERDAS Imagine, IDRISI/TerrSet, Python, and JavaScript to build workflows that are transparent, reproducible, and ready for publication.

As co-founder of GeoCatalog, I provide dedicated research consultancy to PhD scholars and support impactful research publications, alongside hands-on training programs in modern geospatial techniques. Every model I ship is designed to survive peer review — validated, documented, and reproducible.

Cloud-based platforms like Google Earth Engine mean the study area can be anywhere on Earth — I work with scholars and teams remotely across countries and time zones just as closely as I would in person.

Services

Nine ways to work together

From study-area design through GeoAI deployment — consultancy built for theses, journal submissions, and applied planning work.

01 · AOI

Study-Area Design & Mapping

Study-area delineation using boundaries, eco-regions, and custom spatial criteria.

  • Publication-ready base maps: legend, scale, north arrow, insets
  • Multi-source integration — Landsat, Sentinel, DEMs, climate grids, socio-economic data
02 · RS

Remote Sensing & Image Processing

Optical, thermal, and SAR data strategy and preprocessing.

  • Radiometric, atmospheric, geometric correction, mosaicking, enhancement
  • NDVI / EVI / NDWI / NDBI / NDMI index generation, orthorectified classification
03 · LULC

LULC Classification, Change & Prediction

ML-based mapping in ArcGIS Pro, GEE, and QGIS SCP.

  • Confusion matrices, kappa statistics, publication-grade accuracy assessment
  • Future scenarios via CA-Markov, MOLUSCE, LCM & GeoAI prediction
04 · UHI

Urban Heat Island & Climate Analytics

LST retrieval and UHI hotspot mapping from thermal sensors.

  • Blue-green cooling-distance and cooling-gradient analysis
  • Heat-wave risk, exposure & neighbourhood vulnerability mapping
05 · HAB

Biodiversity, Habitat & Mountain Systems

Habitat suitability using optical, SAR, terrain & climate variables.

  • Corridor and fragmentation analysis for wildlife movement
  • Glacier, glacial-lake, GLOF and landslide susceptibility mapping
06 · ENV

Environmental, Climate & Disaster Analysis & Modeling

Earth-science geospatial analysis across land, coast, and ocean systems.

  • Climate-impact analysis on land cover, ecosystems & cities
  • Flood, landslide, drought & multi-hazard risk modeling
  • Shoreline and coastal-change analysis using DSAS, erosion/accretion mapping
  • Ocean and coastal studies — SST, coastal upwelling, wetland & estuarine dynamics
  • Pollution, deforestation & carbon-sequestration monitoring, SDG-aligned
07 · AI

GeoAI & Predictive Modelling

Custom GeoAI for classification, regression, detection & change.

  • Uncertainty analysis and explainable feature importance
  • Scalable cloud pipelines for continuous monitoring
08 · WEB

Web GIS, Dashboards & Automation

GeoServer web GIS to publish maps and layers online.

  • Interactive dashboards combining indicators, charts, time-series
  • Automated GEE/Python pipelines for recurring reporting
09 · EDU

Training, Mentoring & Publication Support

One-to-one and group training in GIS, RS, GeoAI, and GEE.

  • Methodology design and figures aligned to journal standards
  • End-to-end support — proposal through publication
